Prodigy comes under the umbrella of Parousia. It is national level fest organized by Jain College of BBA, BCA & Commerce, Department of BCA. Every year it is organized for 2 days with different events like Cyber-Security, Communication, Gaming, Coding, Best Manager, Designing and Cultural Activities. Teams from In and Around Belagavi and other state participate in the event actively.

Rules & Regulations

  • Maximum Participants 11+1 faculty.
  • Number of Participating teams from each college is limited 2.
  • Registration Fees Rs.2000/-(Local College) & Rs.3000/-(Outstation Colleges)
  • Organizing committee reserves all right to make change in events & dates/schedule.
  • All the Participants must compulsorily carry their college ID cards.
  • Accommodation shall be provided for outstation teams only for 1 day.
  • Judges Decision shall be final & binding.
  • If the team/participant found to be indiscipline or violating the code of conduct leads to disqualification.
  • Kindly maintain Decency and Decorum. Your Co-operation is the key to success.
  • All Participants are responsible for their belongings, College is not responsible for any loss.
